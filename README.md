# Arctic Drift Duck

Arctic Drift Duck is a 3D endless runner where you control a duck riding a skateboard across an icy track. The duck moves left and right with your mouse while obstacles rush toward the camera, creating the feeling of fast forward motion. Your goal is to dodge rocks and penguin hazards for as long as possible, increase your score, and survive as the game speed ramps up over time. When you crash, the run ends and you can restart to try for a higher score.
